Class: IIJRenderer

enioka.ij. IIJRenderer

Will handle rendering with two modules : -HTMLRenderer who gives a HTML value at the element of the head of columns and rows. -HTMLEventHandler who gives a HTML class and id at the head of columns and rows. The renderer has all the specifics functions for create the Headers of the columns and rows and access/modify HTMLclass/CSSProperty.
